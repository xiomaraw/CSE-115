Los Angeles Animal Intake Analysis (2011-2013)

This website is a collection of animal intake data from the Department of Animal Services in Los Angeles from 2011 to 2013. This data was retrieved from data.gov.

Data Analysis

Amount of Animals Admitted per Year

Displays the total amount of animals admitted into an animal shelter in the years 2011, 2012, and 2013.

Amount of Animals Admitted in a Los Angeles Shelter per a Given Year

Displays how many times an animal is admitted into one of Los Angeles Animal Shelters (East and West Valley, South and West Los Angeles, Harbor, and North Centra) from 2011 to 2013.
